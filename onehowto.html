Get to know your Quest tab and My Information


Quest Tab

Press the O button to access your Quest tab. The ongoing tab is all of your currently accepted quest from every region (NOTE: Set it to AREA so you know where to go if you're doing the quests).

The main tab is your main questline that you should do the moment you spawn in western guard camp, furthermore this is the most important tab because some quest have pre-requisites. (NOTE: If you find yourself unable to see the quest you might need to do main quests, for instance the free 3 pets in suggested tab. Thank you Pearl Abyss for giving the players free pets this is very helpful.)

As mentioned above the suggested tab is the optional quest that gives you good rewards. I highly recommend doing the inventory quests first so you can carry more items then get the 3 free pets.

The recurring tab is your daily quests that you can do daily and it resets at 24:00 or 12:00 midnight. (NOTE: This is very important for life skillers because it gives contribution points or for short CP. You will learn more about CP later on.)

The new quest tab is related to black desert events it may be a daily quest or one time completion quests like the cliff's weapons for example so be sure to check the official website for events then look here to complete them.(NOTE: Sometimes you can find it in your black spirit as well.)

The quest action on the other hand is a great feature to follow all of your quest lines. 1 is the confirm quest rewards and you don't usually click this instead click the quest title to see the rewards. 2 if you want to forfeit your quest. 3 this gives you the location of your quest so you can see how far it is. 4 this is similar to 3 and the most used action because it autopath you to the location of your quest. (NOTE: This is a huge lifesaver for new player so don't hesitate to use this.)
